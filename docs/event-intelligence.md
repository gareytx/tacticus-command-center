# Event intelligence

Legendary Event counters and lanes are retained as normalized evidence. Local event objectives, priorities, blockers, deadlines, and preferred teams may support review or planning recommendations. The engine does not infer dates, eligibility, rewards, required units, final score, or unlock success.

See [campaign and event semantics](./campaign-event-semantics.md) and [recommendation semantics](./phase-3a-recommendation-semantics.md).
