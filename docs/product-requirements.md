# Product requirements

## Purpose

Maintain an accurate personal character roster and translate it into actionable progression and team-planning views.

## Release-one capabilities

- Dashboard summaries for ownership, alliance, rarity, priority, recent changes, and active goals.
- Searchable, filterable card and table roster views.
- Validated character creation, editing, detail, and deletion.
- Upgrade goals grouped by priority with lifecycle statuses.
- Mode-specific teams with five primaries and five optional substitutes.
- Versioned JSON export and preview-first, backup-protected import.
- Editable demo data containing the 11 requested owned characters and excluding Ulf.

## Non-goals

Accounts, payments, public profiles, hosted synchronization, external APIs, copyrighted assets, and screenshot recognition are not part of this release.

## Quality bar

All mutations are server-validated, database access is isolated, destructive actions confirm intent, empty and error states are meaningful, and primary workflows work on mobile and desktop.
